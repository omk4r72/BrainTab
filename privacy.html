Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 30, 2026

At BrainTab, your privacy is our top priority. We understand that your browser history is highly personal, and we have built BrainTab with privacy-first principles.

1. Information We Collect

BrainTab is an AI-powered extension designed to act as your second brain. To function, it processes:

  • Page Content: Text and metadata of the active tabs you browse to generate summaries and extract key concepts.
  • Knowledge Graph Data: The relationships and tags generated from your browsing activity.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is strictly used to provide the BrainTab experience:

  • To power the Kira AI assistant, allowing you to ask questions about your tabs.
  • To generate the visual Knowledge Graph so you can see connections between topics.
  • To enable intelligent searching across your history.

3. Local Storage & Processing

Your data is yours. The majority of processing and storage happens entirely locally on your device. BrainTab does not sell, rent, or share your browsing history with third-party advertisers.

If cloud AI models are utilized for summarization, only the minimal required text is transmitted securely via standard encryption protocols (HTTPS/TLS) and is not used to train global AI models.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party AI APIs (like OpenAI) to process page text for summarization. In these cases, data is transmitted strictly for the immediate request and is subject to the strict data privacy agreements of those providers, which prohibit training on API data.

5. Your Choices

You have full control over your data. You can:

  • Clear your BrainTab knowledge graph at any time from the extension settings.
  • Disable BrainTab on specific sensitive websites.
  • Uninstall the extension, which immediately removes access to any locally stored data.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository: github.com/omk4r72/BrainTab.
